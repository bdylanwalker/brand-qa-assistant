## Logo

- Only use approved logo files from the brand portal.
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the logo mark on all sides.
- Do not recolour, stretch, rotate, or add effects to the logo.
- Approved logo variants: full colour (on white/light), reversed (on dark/brand colour).

## Tone of Voice

- Friendly, confident, and human.
- Use active voice; avoid passive constructions.
- Do not use jargon or internal acronyms in customer-facing copy.
- Use sentence case for headings (not Title Case).

## Terminology

- Product name: [Product Name] — always capitalised exactly as shown.
- Do not abbreviate product names.
- Trademark symbols: use ™ on first mention per page; no need to repeat.

## Imagery

- Photography style: natural lighting, authentic people, avoid stock-photo clichés.
- Avoid illustrations with more than 3 brand colours.

## Layout

- Always use a 12-column grid with 24px gutters.
- Primary CTA buttons must have at least 44px height and sufficient colour contrast (WCAG AA).
